SDO Santa Rosa City Accredited as CPD Provider

The Schools Division Office (SDO) of Santa Rosa City has been officially accredited as a Continuing Professional Development (CPD) Provider by the CPD Council of Professional Teachers (Accreditation No. PTR-2024-611). This accreditation allows SDO to offer CPD programs aimed at enhancing the teaching skills and competencies of educators and personnel, supporting their continuous professional growth. The accreditation was led by Mr. Mouller Lumague and Jeanne Elaine Sangalang from the Human Resource Development Section (HRDS), with strong backing from the Division’s Top Management.
