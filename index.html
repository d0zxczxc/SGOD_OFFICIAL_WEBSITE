Planning and Research Section
Collaborative Planning for Education
Continuous Improvement in Schools

The Planning and Research Section of the Schools Division Office (SDO) Santa Rosa City is dedicated to data-driven decision-making, strategic planning, and educational research. We ensure the effective implementation of programs and policies that enhance the quality of education in the city. Through comprehensive data analysis, research initiatives, and collaborative planning, we support the continuous improvement of schools, teachers, and students, fostering a culture of excellence and innovation in education.
