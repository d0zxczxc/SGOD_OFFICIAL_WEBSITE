The Planning and Research Section of the Schools Division Office (SDO) Santa Rosa City is committed to improving the quality of education through data-driven decision-making, strategic planning, and educational research. We aim to ensure the effective implementation of programs and policies that contribute to the betterment of the educational system in our city.

Our primary focus is on comprehensive data analysis, research initiatives, and collaborative planning to support continuous improvement within schools, teachers, and students. We strive to foster a culture of excellence and innovation in education while embracing the importance of teamwork and partnership in achieving our goals.
