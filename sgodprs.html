Planning and Research
Section



The Planning and Research Section (PRS) of the Schools Division Office of Santa Rosa City plays an important role in supporting evidence-based decision-making and strategic planning in education. Through careful data collection, planning, and regular review, PRS helps make sure that programs and policies meet the changing needs of learners, teachers, and schools. The section leads the development of education plans, conducts useful research, and helps track performance to guide improvements. By promoting the use of data and new ideas, PRS helps ensure that every learner has access to quality and inclusive basic education.

The Planning and Research Section of the Schools Division Office (SDO) Santa Rosa City is committed to using data to inform decisions, drive strategic planning, and conduct educational research. Our goal is to effectively implement programs and policies that enhance the educational quality in the city. Through detailed data analysis, research initiatives, and teamwork in planning, we contribute to the continuous improvement of schools, educators, and students, fostering a culture of excellence and innovation in education.
