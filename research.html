Action and Basic Research


The Action and Basic Research section showcases ongoing and completed research projects aimed at improving the educational system. These projects are designed to explore new methods, validate existing strategies, and provide data-driven insights into education reform.

Our focus is on both Action Research (which aims to solve real-world issues) and Basic Research (which contributes to the foundational understanding of educational theories and practices). Through these efforts, we continue to improve the learning environment and educational outcomes for both teachers and students.

The Planning and Research Section of the Schools Division Office (SDO) Santa Rosa City is committed to using data to inform decisions, drive strategic planning, and conduct educational research. Our goal is to effectively implement programs and policies that enhance the educational quality in the city. Through detailed data analysis, research initiatives, and teamwork in planning, we contribute to the continuous improvement of schools, educators, and students, fostering a culture of excellence and innovation in education.
